What We Do

Full-Range Trade Services

Barry Dell Plumbing has expertise in all aspects of plumbing, drainage and heating. All services are performed by first-class, fully trained and certified experts. All clients, regardless of the size or service performed, can be assured they will receive a friendly, reliable and cost-effective solution.

🔧 Plumbing

Emergency repairs, kitchen & bathroom upgrades, new homes, commercial buildings, water filters, marine servicing, water mains and spouting. Domestic & commercial.

🚿 Drainage

Drain clearing, hydro jet cleaning, CCTV drain inspection, excavation, new drainage work, storm water sumps and vacuum/suction trucks. Domestic & commercial.

🏗 Roofing

New roofs, re-roofing, repairs, flashings, roof cleaning, spouting, gutters. Licensed installers of Viking Membrane Roofing — Enviroclad, Butylclad & Dec-K-ing.

🪣 Gutter Cleaning

Domestic and commercial spouting and gutter cleaning by trained industrial abseiling specialists. Programmed and routine roof maintenance available.

🔍 Thermal Imaging

Locate moisture problems, identify hidden leaks and detect heat loss and poor insulation — before small issues become expensive ones.

🔥 Heating & Gasfitting

Radiators, ventilation, valving, pumps and routine maintenance. Gas fitting for heating, cooking, hot water — residential and commercial installation.

Our Story

The Plumbers
by the Brook

For more than 50 years, Barry Dell Plumbing has been a trusted name in and around the Otago area. Barry and Liz Carmichael took over ownership in August 2001 and have since grown the team from just 3 staff to 25 qualified tradespeople.

Based at the old Glen Motor Court on the corner of Glen and South Roads, the team has been affectionately known as the "Plumbers by the Brook" — named after their proximity to Carisbrook, the old "House of Pain".
